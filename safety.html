Safety

Safety when at home

Scopd protects your location when you are in your residential zipcode. Thus, users cannot know where you live via our app.

Safety with age

Scopd has age restrictions built into the application to prevent unsavory usage of the app. Scopd is not the place for ANY SUCH ACTIONS on behalf of the youth or a person of older age.

Clear intentions

Say goodbye to the days when someone desired to connect but was unclear about their intentions. We require users to make clear their intentions up front, including why they would like to connect in the first place.

Free from harassment

We empower users to block anyone they feel uncomfortable with or threatened by.
